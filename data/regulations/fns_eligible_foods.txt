FNS SNAP Eligible Food Items Guide
Food and Nutrition Service, USDA

OVERVIEW OF SNAP ELIGIBLE FOODS

SNAP benefits can be used to buy food for the household to eat, such as:
- Breads and cereals
- Fruits and vegetables
- Meats, fish, and poultry
- Dairy products
- Seeds and plants, which produce food for the household to eat

DETAILED ELIGIBLE CATEGORIES

1. BREAD AND CEREALS
   - All types of bread (white, wheat, multigrain, specialty)
   - Breakfast cereals (hot and cold)
   - Rice, pasta, noodles
   - Flour, cornmeal, grits
   - Tortillas, pita bread, naan
   - Crackers and breadsticks
   - Oatmeal and granola
   - Baking mixes (cake, pancake, muffin)

2. FRUITS AND VEGETABLES
   - Fresh fruits and vegetables
   - Canned fruits and vegetables
   - Frozen fruits and vegetables
   - Dried fruits
   - 100% fruit and vegetable juices
   - Pre-cut and packaged produce
   - Organic produce

3. MEATS, FISH, AND POULTRY
   - Fresh meat (beef, pork, lamb, veal)
   - Fresh poultry (chicken, turkey, duck)
   - Fresh fish and seafood
   - Canned meat, poultry, and fish
   - Frozen meat, poultry, and fish
   - Deli meats and cold cuts (not hot/heated)
   - Shellfish (shrimp, crab, lobster, oysters)

4. DAIRY PRODUCTS
   - Milk (whole, reduced fat, skim, flavored)
   - Cheese (all varieties)
   - Yogurt
   - Butter and margarine
   - Cream and sour cream
   - Eggs
   - Plant-based milk alternatives (soy, almond, oat, etc.)

5. SNACK FOODS AND TREATS
   - Potato chips, corn chips, tortilla chips
   - Candy and chocolate
   - Cookies and cakes
   - Ice cream and frozen desserts
   - Popsicles and frozen treats
   - Pudding and gelatin desserts
   - Popcorn

6. BEVERAGES
   - Soft drinks (soda, cola)
   - Energy drinks with Nutrition Facts label
   - Sports drinks
   - Coffee (ground, instant, pods)
   - Tea (loose leaf, bags, instant)
   - Bottled water
   - Juice drinks and fruit punches

7. CONDIMENTS AND COOKING SUPPLIES
   - Salt, pepper, spices, herbs
   - Cooking oils and sprays
   - Vinegar
   - Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise
   - Salad dressings
   - Soy sauce, hot sauce
   - Baking powder, baking soda, yeast
   - Sugar and sweeteners

8. BABY FOOD AND FORMULA
   - Infant formula
   - Baby cereals
   - Jarred baby food
   - Baby snacks and puffs
   - Toddler meals

9. SEEDS AND PLANTS
   - Vegetable seeds (tomato, pepper, lettuce, etc.)
   - Herb seeds (basil, cilantro, parsley)
   - Fruit-bearing plants (strawberry, tomato plants)
   - Food-producing seeds and plants only

10. SPECIALTY ITEMS
    - Gluten-free foods
    - Kosher foods
    - Halal foods
    - Organic foods
    - Natural and health foods with Nutrition Facts labels

COLD PREPARED FOODS (ELIGIBLE)

The following cold prepared foods ARE eligible because they are intended for home consumption:
- Deli sandwiches (cold)
- Cold pizza (refrigerated)
- Cold salads from deli
- Sushi (cold)
- Cold fried chicken
- Refrigerated prepared meals
