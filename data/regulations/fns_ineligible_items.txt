FNS SNAP Ineligible Items Guide
Food and Nutrition Service, USDA

ITEMS NOT ELIGIBLE FOR SNAP PURCHASE

SNAP benefits CANNOT be used to buy:

1. ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES
   - Beer (including non-alcoholic beer with any alcohol content)
   - Wine (including cooking wine if labeled as alcoholic)
   - Liquor and spirits
   - Malt beverages
   - Hard seltzers
   - Wine coolers
   - Any beverage containing alcohol

   Note: Products labeled as "non-alcoholic" may still contain trace amounts of alcohol (up to 0.5% ABV) and their eligibility may vary by state interpretation.

2. TOBACCO PRODUCTS
   - Cigarettes
   - Cigars and cigarillos
   - Pipe tobacco
   - Chewing tobacco and snuff
   - E-cigarettes and vaping devices
   - Vape juice and pods (with or without nicotine)
   - Nicotine patches, gums, and lozenges

3. VITAMINS, MEDICINES, AND SUPPLEMENTS
   Any product with a SUPPLEMENT FACTS label is NOT eligible, including:
   - Multivitamins
   - Individual vitamins (Vitamin C, D, B12, etc.)
   - Mineral supplements (iron, calcium, zinc)
   - Herbal supplements
   - Protein powders with Supplement Facts label
   - Fish oil and omega-3 supplements
   - Probiotics sold as supplements
   - Energy supplements
   - Weight loss supplements
   - Sports nutrition supplements

   IMPORTANT DISTINCTION:
   - Nutrition Facts label = FOOD (potentially eligible)
   - Supplement Facts label = SUPPLEMENT (NOT eligible)

4. HOT FOODS AND HOT FOOD PRODUCTS
   Foods that are hot at the point of sale are NOT eligible:
   - Rotisserie chicken
   - Hot pizza
   - Hot dogs from a warmer
   - Hot soup or chili
   - Hot coffee or tea (prepared)
   - Hot breakfast items
   - Foods from steam tables
   - Heated prepared meals

   Exception: Food that is sold cold/room temperature but will be heated by the consumer at home IS eligible.

5. FOODS PREPARED FOR IMMEDIATE CONSUMPTION
   - Restaurant meals
   - Fast food
   - Food court purchases
   - Cafeteria meals
   - Any food intended to be eaten on premises

6. NON-FOOD ITEMS
   - Pet foods (dog food, cat food, fish food, etc.)
   - Cleaning supplies
   - Paper products (toilet paper, paper towels)
   - Personal hygiene items (soap, shampoo, toothpaste)
   - Cosmetics and beauty products
   - Household supplies
   - Diapers
   - Medicines and pharmaceuticals
   - First aid supplies

7. LIVE ANIMALS
   The following are NOT eligible:
   - Live chickens
   - Live fish (while still in water/alive)
   - Live cattle, pigs, or other livestock
   - Live rabbits
   - Any animal that is alive at point of sale

   EXCEPTIONS (These ARE eligible):
   - Shellfish removed from tank (clams, mussels, oysters)
   - Fish removed from water
   - Animals slaughtered prior to customer pick-up

8. CBD AND CANNABIS PRODUCTS
   - CBD-infused foods and beverages
   - CBD oils and tinctures
   - Hemp-derived CBD products
   - Marijuana edibles
   - THC-containing products
   - Any cannabis-derived food product

   Note: Even in states where cannabis is legal, these products are NOT SNAP-eligible under federal law.

9. PREPARED FOOD WITH HOT SALES THRESHOLD
   Establishments where more than 50% of sales are hot prepared food are classified as restaurants and their items are generally ineligible.

10. GIFT BASKETS AND NON-FOOD COMBINATIONS
    - Gift baskets containing non-food items
    - Products bundled with non-food items where the non-food portion has significant value

SPECIAL NOTES

Energy Drinks:
- Energy drinks with a NUTRITION FACTS label ARE eligible
- Energy drinks with a SUPPLEMENT FACTS label are NOT eligible
- Check the label carefully - some energy drinks are classified as supplements

Protein Bars and Shakes:
- Products with NUTRITION FACTS labels ARE eligible
- Products with SUPPLEMENT FACTS labels are NOT eligible
- Many protein products fall into the supplement category

Meal Replacement Drinks:
- Ensure, Boost, and similar products with NUTRITION FACTS labels ARE eligible
- Similar products marketed as supplements with SUPPLEMENT FACTS labels are NOT eligible

Source: https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/eligible-food-items
